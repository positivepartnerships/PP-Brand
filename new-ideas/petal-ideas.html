Exploring the Petal Motif

Ideas for evolving the Diversity Wheel's central bloom into a flexible brand shape system — for slides, web, print, and beyond. Each concept here is derived from the overlapping petal forms at the heart of the wheel.

The Source: Diversity Wheel

The central bloom is made of overlapping, translucent petal shapes radiating from a single point. Each petal is a different hue — together they form a complete spectrum. This is the visual metaphor we're extracting from.

What it represents: Every petal is unique in colour and position, but they overlap and connect to create something whole. That's neurodiversity. That's partnership.

Petal Clusters

Groups of 3–7 overlapping petals that echo the bloom without being the full wheel. These work as corner accents, card decorations, or background elements.
